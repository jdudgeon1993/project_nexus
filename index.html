🚌 Free Downtown Buses

Click on a bus route to see more details

🚌

16th Street FreeRide

The Explorer

Running
▼
🎯
Best For
Sightseeing, dining, and reaching specific shops on the pedestrian mall
📍
Where It Goes
Travels directly up and down the 16th Street Mall. Stops at every single intersection - you never walk more than half a block!
📅
When It Runs
Daily: 7 days a week, 365 days a year
Hours: 5:00 AM - 1:20 AM
⏱️
Frequency
Every 3-10 minutes
💡
Pro Tip: This is a "hop-on, hop-off" service. Because it's free and stops every block, it's the easiest way for first-timers to navigate the main tourist corridor without a map.
🚌

Free MetroRide

The Express

Off Hours
▼
🎯
Best For
Getting across downtown quickly during work hours without the "stop-and-go" of the MallRide
📍
Where It Goes
Runs parallel to the mall:
18th Street → Civic Center
19th Street → Union Station
Only 7 stops each way
📅
When It Runs
Days: Monday - Friday only
Morning Rush: 6:00 AM - 9:00 AM
Afternoon Rush: 3:00 PM - 6:30 PM
⏱️
Frequency
Every 10 minutes during rush hours
💡
Pro Tip: Look for large white canopies at "Super Stops" designed for fast boarding. If you're in a hurry to catch a train at Union Station during afternoon rush, this is much faster than the 16th Street shuttle.
